Crop Management Actions

Users can perform the following actions related to crop management:

  • Add New Crop: Enter details to add a new crop to the system.
  • Edit Crop Information: Modify existing crop details for accuracy.
  • Delete Crop: Remove a crop that is no longer cultivated.

Monitoring Crop Health

Regular monitoring of crop health is crucial for optimal yield. Key indicators to observe include:

  • Pest and Disease Levels
  • Growth Stages
  • Water Requirements
  • Fertilization Needs

For detailed analytics and assistance, please contact the crop management team.
