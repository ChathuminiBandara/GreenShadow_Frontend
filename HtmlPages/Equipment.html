Equipment Management Actions

Users can perform the following actions related to equipment management:

  • Add New Equipment: Enter details to add new equipment to the system.
  • Edit Equipment Information: Modify existing equipment details for accuracy.
  • Delete Equipment: Remove equipment that is no longer in use.

Regular Maintenance

Routine maintenance is crucial to ensure the longevity and efficiency of agricultural equipment. Key practices include:

  • Regular Oil Changes
  • Checking Tire Pressure
  • Cleaning Filters
  • Annual Inspections

For any issues or support regarding equipment, please contact the equipment management team.
