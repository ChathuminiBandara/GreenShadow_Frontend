Field Management Actions

Users can perform the following actions related to field management:

  • Add New Field: Enter details to add a new field to the system.
  • Edit Field Information: Modify existing field details for accuracy.
  • Delete Field: Remove a field that is no longer in use.

Field Conditions

Regular monitoring of field conditions is essential for effective crop management. Here are some key indicators to track:

  • Soil Moisture Levels
  • Pest and Disease Alerts
  • Weather Conditions
  • Harvesting Status

For more detailed analytics and management, please contact the field management team.
